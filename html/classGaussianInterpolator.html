Detailed Description

Gaussian interpolator

This class helps to perform a quick evaluation of the N(0,1) Gaussian. 1/sqrt(2*PI)*exp(-x^2)

GaussianInterpolator GI;
GI.initialize(6,60000);
double g=GI.getValue(1.96);
